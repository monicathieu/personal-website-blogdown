Hierarchical bootstrapping

2020-03-19
#tidyverse  #bootstrapping  #simulation 
What’s bootstrapping again? Basic tidy bootstrapping Multilevel data: a real cluster-f Honoring the structure of the data: it matters! There’s a lot of things to be anxious about lately. One of the more harmless questions I worry about is–am I estimating the standard error of my statistics incorrectly? Bootstrapping my standard errors is a great way to tackle that anxiety. However, bootstrapping can get a bit more complex as data take on a more complex structure (for example, multilevel structure), and the way that we resample our data can have big impacts on the variance of the resulting distributions.
